# Implementation Plan: Translating the EU AI Act into Governance-as-Code (YAML)

This plan outlines the approach to translate the regulatory requirements of the **EU AI Act (specifically Articles 8–17 for High-Risk Systems)** into a comprehensive, machine-readable YAML configuration schema that the SIA framework can enforce at runtime.

## ⚖️ Background Context
The EU AI Act mandates strict requirements for high-risk AI systems. While much of the Act involves organizational compliance (e.g., QMS, human resources), our goal is to translate the **technical, runtime-enforceable requirements** into deterministic YAML rules. 

Our Sovereign Stack (Ingress, Traceability, Egress) will execute these rules on every prompt and generation.

## 🛠️ Proposed Changes

### [Component: Governance Config Schema]
We will create a massive, structured YAML file (`configs/eu_ai_act_full.yaml`) that maps directly to the Articles of the EU AI Act.

#### [NEW] `configs/eu_ai_act_full.yaml`
We will structure the YAML hierarchically by EU AI Act Articles, applying per-environment granularity (`environments`) and specific `annex_iii_categories`.

*   **Article 10 (Data and Data Governance)**
    *   *Implementation:* Ingress rules to enforce data sanitization, bias checks, and representation constraints.
    *   *YAML Logic:* `STRIP_PII`, `BLOCK_PROHIBITED_DOMAINS`
*   **Article 12 (Record-Keeping)**
    *   *Implementation:* Traceability rules ensuring all events, inputs, and outputs are logged and cryptographically hashed.
    *   *YAML Logic:* `REQUIRE_TRACEABILITY_HASH`, `LOG_RETENTION_POLICY: 10_YEARS`
*   **Article 13 (Transparency)**
    *   *Implementation:* Egress rule ensuring the output clearly states it was generated by an AI.
    *   *YAML Logic:* `APPEND_WATERMARK("Generated by AI")`
*   **Article 14 (Human Oversight)**
    *   *Implementation:* Detects high-risk Annex III tasks (e.g., hiring, clinical triage) and enforces human-in-the-loop (HITL) gates, returning HTTP 202 Accepted instead of blocking.
    *   *YAML Logic:* `IF task IN [employment, healthcare] THEN REQUIRE_HUMAN_VETO (returns 202)`
*   **Article 15 (Accuracy, Robustness, Cybersecurity)**
    *   *Implementation:* Egress rules enforcing the Truth Razor (hallucination filtering) and minimum confidence thresholds.
    *   *YAML Logic:* `MIN_CONFIDENCE: 0.85`, `REQUIRE_RAG_GROUNDING: true`

### [Component: Core Engine & Parser Updates]
To support the complex EU AI Act schema, the underlying Python engine must be upgraded.

#### [MODIFY] `src/sia/core/config.py`
- Expand `Pydantic` models to support nested structures (e.g., grouping rules by `article` and `annex`).
- Support `environments: ["prod", "staging"]` lists.

#### [MODIFY] `src/sia/core/engine.py`
- Implement a more robust evaluation tree.
- Map the new YAML logic strings to actual Python execution functions in the Ingress and Egress layers.
